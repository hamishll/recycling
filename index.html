Recycling top tips!

1. Hard plastics are generally recyclable at home. Thin films and bags are generally not recyclable at home.

2. Oily food residue left on paper can prevent it from being recycled. Only recycle paper packaging that is/has been cleaned.

3. Composite paper and plastic products, such as food pouches, are generally not recyclable as the plastic coatings can't be separated from the paper.
